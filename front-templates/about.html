About TrashTrack

Welcome to TrashTrack, your dedicated platform for real-time garbage truck tracking. Our mission is to streamline waste management by providing you with live updates on the location and schedule of garbage trucks in your area.

How TrashTrack Works

TrashTrack utilizes cutting-edge technology to monitor the movement of garbage trucks as they navigate through neighborhoods. The process is simple yet powerful: you enter your location, and TrashTrack keeps you informed about when the garbage truck is scheduled to arrive at your doorstep.

Key Features

  • Real-time tracking of garbage trucks
  • Customizable notifications based on your location
  • Efficient waste management for cleaner communities
  • Reliable and timely updates for a hassle-free experience
